Cross Platform with PhoneGap & Framework7

  • Together with the styling built into framework7 and google's firebase authentication with storage and database, I created an application that allows multiple users to post and delete images along with titles and descriptions that are editable. There are profile tabs that allow users to filter out their own posts.
  • Similar to the methods explained above, I created an application meant for storing food diary inputs while tracking the percentage of a persons diet that remains in the health range. Users are able to share their results with personal trainers, who are notified when there in a new input.
  • Through the use of a weather API, I was able to pull from data that is stored in JSON to display forecasts and current weather, based on the changing image that appears as the background.
